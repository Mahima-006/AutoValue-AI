# AutoValue AI — Demo Script / Storyboard

A suggested narration script for your LinkedIn screen-recording demo (≈60–90 seconds).

---

## Scene 1 — Open on the Landing Page (0–10s)

**Screen**: Home page loads. Hero headline fades in with staggered animation.

**Narration**:
> "This is AutoValue AI — an end-to-end ML project I built to predict used-car market values in India. The frontend is built with React and Framer Motion, the backend runs on FastAPI, and the model is a Random Forest trained on real Quikr listing data."

**What to show**: Let the hero animations play out. Point out the "Live Model v4.2" chip and the "40M+ Points" stat card.

---

## Scene 2 — Start Valuation (10–25s)

**Screen**: Click "Start Valuation →". Step 1 slides in from the right.

**Narration**:
> "The app walks you through a 3-step form. First: manufacturer, year, and model name. I'll value a 2019 Hyundai Creta."

**What to show**: Fill in — Manufacturer: Hyundai, Year: 2019, Model: Creta. Hit "Continue →". Step 2 slides in, progress bar animates to 66%.

---

## Scene 3 — Condition & Mileage (25–40s)

**Screen**: Step 2 with mileage slider and condition cards.

**Narration**:
> "Step 2 captures mileage and condition. I'll drag the slider to 45,000 km, select 'Good' condition, and tick Leather Seats."

**What to show**: Drag mileage slider — show the glowing thumb. Click "Good" condition card — show the blue glow border. Check "Leather Seats". Hit "NEXT STEP".

---

## Scene 4 — Review & Submit (40–50s)

**Screen**: Step 3 summary card.

**Narration**:
> "Step 3 shows a full review — location, all specs — then we hit 'Get Valuation' to call the FastAPI backend."

**What to show**: Select Mumbai, briefly scan the summary card, hit "Get Valuation →". Show the spinning loader.

---

## Scene 5 — Results (50–75s)

**Screen**: Results screen loads. Price counts up from ₹0 to final value.

**Narration**:
> "And here's the magic. The model predicts the market value — watch the number count up. We get a confidence score, market volatility rating, and a 12-month price trend chart."

**What to show**:
1. Price count-up animation ← pause here for a moment
2. Confidence bar filling left-to-right
3. Scroll to the Price Trends chart — lines drawing in
4. Comparative Analysis tiles pop in

---

## Scene 6 — Model Metrics Callout (75–90s)

**Screen**: Scroll to "Verified Appraisal" card on Results screen.

**Narration**:
> "The model achieved R² = 63% on a holdout test set across 3 candidate models — Linear Regression, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting. Random Forest won. Not production-grade, but a real, end-to-end ML pipeline — model, API, and UI — all from scratch. Full code is linked below."

**What to show**: Zoom in on the Verified Appraisal card showing the R² figure.
